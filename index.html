Only a true animal lover can
appreciate what it means when
we lose our pets. The emotional
bond continues -- since many
view their pets as valued family
members -- sharing in years of
family activities with
personalities of their own.

For many of us, a very personal
connection continues for years,
as family members share
memories of special times. This
unique urn, in the shape of a
Cross, helps us to honor those
memories as we deal with our
family's loss.
Each Cross Urn comes with a keepsake medal of
St. Francis of Assisi, (shown at left) known
throughout the world as the "Patron of Animals."
It includes the "Prayer of St. Francis on the
reverse side. A brass plate is also included for
you to inscribe for the pet's name and dates.
This beautiful (Shown in Almond)  
measures 11 inches by 7 inches by 5
inches deep and fits most all size
pets. Comes in Almond or Walnut.

The closure (shown left) is secured
by an interlocking design with three
brass latches and brass hinges.
Each package also comes with glue
to secure the remains, as desired.

This is an all-natural wood product,
completely hand-crafted and suitable
for display or burial.
The top (shown at
right) also bears the
deeply incised cross
motif for added
beauty in the design.
